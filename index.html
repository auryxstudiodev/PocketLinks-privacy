Privacy Policy for PocketLinks

Last updated: January 2026

PocketLinks values your privacy and is committed to protecting it.

1. Information Collection and Use

PocketLinks does not collect, store, process, or share any personal data or sensitive user information.

The application works entirely offline. All links saved by the user are stored locally on the user’s device only and are never transmitted to any server or third party.

2. Personal Data

PocketLinks does not require user registration and does not request or collect:

  • Names
  • Email addresses
  • Phone numbers
  • Location data
  • Contacts
  • Photos or media
  • Any other personal or sensitive information

3. Third-Party Services

PocketLinks does not use:

  • Advertising services
  • Analytics tools
  • Tracking technologies
  • Third-party SDKs that collect user data

4. Data Security

Since PocketLinks does not collect or transmit any user data, there is no external data storage or processing involved. All data remains under the user’s control on their own device.

5. Children’s Privacy

PocketLinks does not knowingly collect any data from children under the age of 13. As no data is collected, there is no risk of exposure of children’s personal information.

6. Changes to This Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated revision date.
